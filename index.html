about

I'm Andrew, a software engineer living in Blacksburg, Virginia. I am a student at Virginia Tech and expect to graduate in May of 2017 with a bachelor's degree in computer science and minor in mathematics.

I've been interested in software development ever since I borrowed a Java book from my father's bookshelf on a whim when I was sixteen. Since then, I have discovered a love and passion for the field that I have never felt for anything else.

Since starting school at Tech, I have taken many computer science classes and, more importantly, worked two different jobs in the field. During the summer of 2014, I worked as a researcher for IT Security Lab here on campus. The following year, I was a software engineering intern for a company called Solers, Inc and will be returning there this May. There is a link to my complete resume at the bottom of the page.

Over the years, I have worked on many small projects on my own time, though I rarely create anything presentable. It is my hope that creating this site as a sort of portfolio will inspire me to begin working on more polished and interesting projects.

Outside of computer science, I am an Eagle Scout and avid rock climber. I also enjoy being outdoors in general, running, and cooking.
